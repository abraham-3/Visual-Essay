Is Brutalism the answer?



A “raw focus on content, and prioritization of the website visitor”, through basic interfaces components.

An interfaces that puts content first, and uses decorations only if required to support it.

Inspired by web design from the 90s, with the goal of creating unambiguous designs.

"To trick or deceive the visitor goes against the nature of a website, which is to deliver information and interact honestly with a visitor."

David Copeland - Engineering Director at Stitch Fix
