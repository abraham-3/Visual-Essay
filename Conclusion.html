So … what defines a good UI Design?

"[A] Great UI is like promising a user that they will feel cozy and comfortable and they can solve their tasks easily and fast here.”

Vladislav Gavriluk (Arounda)

“Our daily lives are reliant on sophisticated layers of tech which is necessarily robotic and clunky.

The opportunity for UI Designers is to help people understand and trust the underlying tech through product experiences while embodying the values of brands constructing them”

Nathan Burazer (Eventbrite)

“[In UI Design] You’re bound to the constraints of tried-and-true best practices and accessibility standards, but you have opportunities to flex your creative muscles in a multitude of ways.

The ability to find those unique opportunities is often what defines great UI design.”

Edith Goulet (Plato Design)

Conclusion

Design trends encourage designers to explore new visual aesthetics and patterns, by developing their skill and knowledge...

...but these trends should always aid the end goal, of creating something usable, meaningful and human.
